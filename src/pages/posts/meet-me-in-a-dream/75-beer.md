<pre>
			-- In honor of 
			   Jingu Stadium

Matsoka's home runs are not
          hit for me
—— So the unhappy
          beer vendor tells us
</pre>

1981/5/16 <br>
from "An Anthology of Yakult Swallows Poetry"
